What is OurEye.ai?

OurEye.ai is an automated end to end security and management system for residential Apartments, Gated communities, Hotels and Restaurants, Offices, corporations and factories.

  • The system is completely automated and requires minimal maintenance and human intervention.
  • OurEye.ai uses Artificial Intelligence technology and automatically takes care of your security using cctv cameras.

How does it work?

  • Data Capture: Easy Data Capture through Person Recognition, Uncivilized activity Detection, Vehicle Log through License Plate.
  • Scalable Model: Integrate with Existing CCTV or New Camera setup i.e. supplement to CCTV or New Setup.
  • Macro & Micro View: Dashboard Info for Entire Property and Unit Property.
  • Real-Time Notifications: Customisable Notification Rules and Respond/Request help near you.
  • Need based Insights: Get insights of what is happening in your society.

Features

Our solution has two interfaces: Resident/Owner Mobile Application view & Security and management Dashboard

Resident or Employee Safety

Notifications & Alerts triggering in case of conflicts and accidents with Perimeter Control.

Real time logging

Identification of known/unknown person & Check whether person is authorized over time or before time.

OneShot Model Training

Registration of People with One Shot. Reinforcement Learning over time through camera events.

Object Control

Vehicle Entry & Exit Log with notifications. Uncivilized Activity such as littering, smoking (No Smoke Zones) & Public Defection.
